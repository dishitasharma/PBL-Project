Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our feedback system

How is my feedback processed? +

All feedback is reviewed by the relevant department within 48 hours. Critical issues are escalated to department heads immediately. You will receive updates on the status of your feedback through email notifications.

Can I submit anonymous feedback? +

Yes, you can choose to submit feedback anonymously by checking the "Submit anonymously" option in the feedback form. Please note that while your identity will be protected, providing contact information can help us follow up on your feedback if needed.

How long does it take to address feedback? +

The timeline varies depending on the nature of the feedback. Simple issues may be resolved within days, while more complex matters might take several weeks. The current average resolution time is 7 days, and you can track the status of your feedback using your feedback ID.
